# What is Automotive SBOM?  
*Automotive SBOM* is a guideline for describing software composition information in a standardized format for the automotive industry.
Being used in suppliers as a common guideline for creating SBOMs that meet quality criteria, Automotive SBOM  improve transparency and traceability throughout our supply chain.

# Our Mission
Our mission is to accelerate smart and effective SBOM usage in the automotive industry. SBOMs are critical for improving transparency, security, and compliance across the automotive software supply chain. However, widespread adoption requires overcoming several challenges.  
To achieve this mission, we aim to:
- Identify key challenges, concerns, and barriers that hinder effective SBOM adoption within the automotive ecosystem.
- Understand the needs of OEMs, suppliers, and other stakeholders to ensure SBOM practices are practical and scalable.
